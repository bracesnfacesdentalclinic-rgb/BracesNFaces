Who We Are

Excellence in Dental Care & Facial Aesthetics

Since 2009, Braces N Faces Dental Clinic has been a trusted name in advanced dental and facial aesthetic care in Rajajinagar, Bengaluru. Founded and led by specialists Dr. Roopak Mathew David and Dr. Beena Roopak, our clinic integrates cutting-edge technology with a compassionate, patient-first approach.

We focus on lasting outcomes, creating personalised treatment journeys that enhance oral health, facial harmony, and natural confidence.

Meet the Specialists

The Faces Behind Your Smile

Dr. Roopak Mathew David

MDS, FWFO (USA)

Board-Certified Orthodontist & Premium Invisalign Provider

Read Full Profile

Dr. Roopak Mathew David is an experienced Orthodontist who completed his postgraduate training in Orthodontics from the prestigious A.B. Shetty Memorial Institute of Dental Sciences.

He is a Fellow of the World Federation of Orthodontists (FWFO) and a member of the American Association of Orthodontists (AAO) and the Indian Orthodontic Society.

With over 18+ years of clinical experience, Dr. Roopak was among the early Invisalign providers in Bengaluru, having received advanced Invisalign training in 2016 when clear aligner therapy was still in its early stages of adoption in India.

Specialised Expertise

  • Invisalign/ Clear Aligners
  • Damon / Self-Ligating Braces
  • Lingual Orthodontics
  • Dentofacial Orthopaedics
  • Aesthetic & Adult Orthodontics

An All India Rank holder in the Postgraduate Dental Entrance Examination (2004), he has contributed to several national and international publications and presented scientific papers at orthodontic forums.

Prof. Dr. Beena Roopak, MDS

Oral & Maxillofacial Surgeon | Implantologist

Read Full Profile

Prof. Dr. Beena Roopak completed her Master’s degree in Oral & Maxillofacial Surgery from Yenepoya Dental College and Hospital and currently serves as a Professor at Rajarajeshwari Dental College and Hospital.

She is a Fellow of the International Congress of Oral Implantologists (ICOI) and a certified Implantologist trained in Nobel Biocare implant systems, with advanced expertise in dental implantology and full-mouth rehabilitation.

She has undergone intensive training in cleft rhinoplasty, facial trauma management, and advanced reconstructive procedures at Ampang Hospital, Malaysia.

Clinical Expertise

  • Oral & Maxillofacial Surgery
  • Dental Implantology
  • Facial Trauma Management
  • Cleft Lip & Palate Surgery
  • Rhinoplasty (Nose Correction)
  • Facial Aesthetic & Reconstructive Procedures

Professional Memberships

  • Fellow, International Congress of Oral Implantologists (ICOI)
  • Member, Association of Oral and Maxillofacial Surgeons of India (AOMSI)
  • Member, Indian Dental Association (IDA)

Academic & Professional Experience

  • 18+ years of clinical experience in Oral & Maxillofacial Surgery
  • Published research articles in national and international scientific journals
  • Delivered guest lectures and academic presentations at national forums.
