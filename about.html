Who We Are

Excellence in Dental Care & Facial Aesthetics

Since 2009, Braces N Faces Dental Clinic has been a trusted name in advanced dental and facial aesthetic care in Rajajinagar, Bengaluru. Founded and led by specialists Dr. Roopak Mathew David and Dr. Beena Roopak, our clinic integrates cutting-edge technology with a compassionate, patient-first approach.

We don't just treat symptoms; we design personalised treatment journeys that enhance both oral health and facial harmony, delivering natural, confident results.

Meet the Specialists

The Faces Behind Your Smile

Dr. Roopak Mathew David

BDS, MDS (Orthodontics & Dentofacial Orthopaedics)

Orthodontist & Premium Invisalign Provider

Read Full Profile

Dr. Roopak Mathew David is a highly skilled Orthodontist and Dentofacial Orthopaedic specialist with over 15 years of clinical experience. He completed his BDS and MDS from Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences, Karnataka.

  • Premium Invisalign Provider
  • Fellow of the World Federation of Orthodontists (FWFO)
  • Life Member of the Indian Orthodontic Society (IOS)
  • Member of the Indian Dental Association (IDA)

Dr. Roopak specialises in interceptive orthodontics for growing children, clear aligners (Invisalign), self-ligating braces (Damon System), and complex dentofacial deformity corrections.

Dr. Beena Roopak

BDS, MDS (Oral & Maxillofacial Surgery)

Oral & Maxillofacial Surgeon | Implantologist

Read Full Profile

Dr. Beena Roopak is an expert Oral and Maxillofacial Surgeon and Implantologist, known for her precision and gentle surgical approach. She holds a BDS and MDS from Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences.

  • Fellow of the International Congress of Oral Implantologists (FICOI)
  • Life Member of the Association of Oral and Maxillofacial Surgeons of India (AOMSI)

Her expertise spans across complex dental implants, full mouth rehabilitations, wisdom tooth surgeries, facial trauma management, and advanced facial aesthetic procedures including Botox and dermal fillers.
