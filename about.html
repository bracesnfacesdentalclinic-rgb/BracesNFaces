Who We Are

A Practice Built on Trust & Expertise

Navarang Dental Clinic & Maxillofacial Centre is a multi-speciality dental practice in the heart of Rajajinagar, Bengaluru. Led by gold medallist dental surgeons Dr. Monisha and Dr. Sanjeev, our clinic combines advanced medical technology with a warm, patient-centered approach that puts your comfort and confidence first.

We are proud to be an inclusive, LGBTQ+ friendly, and women-owned practice — a space where every patient feels safe, respected, and genuinely cared for.
